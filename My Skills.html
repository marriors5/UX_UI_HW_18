My UX Skills

User Research

Understanding what the original concept is and then pushing forward to see how the users want it to be morphed, gathering their thoughts and ideas to build a better system based off it.

Empathy

I approach with a friendly manner, I learn about the individual at first, learning how they are, what gets them up, what gets them bothered and then connect with them to answer their problems.

Collaboration

I introduce myself and I start a small conversation, I learn from my groupmates to understand their way of thinking. I figure out what are their strengths and weaknesses, applying them in the best manner to the situation or project. If people are feeling troubled or have a challenge, I will discuss it with them and I help the both of us understand what to do.
